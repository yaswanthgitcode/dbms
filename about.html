About Life Flow

Life Flow is a blood donation management system designed to connect donors and recipients instantly. We aim to make blood donation fast, safe, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Every 2 seconds, someone in India needs blood. Our mission is to bridge the gap between donors and those in need by creating a smart, digital platform that empowers people to help each other in real-time.

We believe in community-driven impact. With your support, we can ensure no life is lost due to lack of blood availability.

How It Works

  • Donors register with their blood type and location.
  • Recipients can search for nearby donors in seconds.
  • Secure and simple contact for quick action.
  • Track donation history and manage your availability.
